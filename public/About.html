Purpose/Goal

Our primary goal is to address the escalating crime rates in PG County, specifically focusing on incidents occurring from July 2023 to the present day. We aim to develop a robust system that not only provides real-time alerts to users but also offers valuable insights into the most prevalent crimes across different districts of the county. By doing so, our purpose is to empower residents and visitors, enhancing their safety and overall sense of security.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to leverage technology to create a safer environment for the residents and visitors of PG County. We are committed to delivering real-time information on crime incidents, allowing users to stay informed and make educated decisions about their safety. Through our innovative application, we strive to contribute to the reduction of crime rates and foster a community that is both informed and proactive in addressing security concerns.

What to Expect

On our home page, expect an interactive experience featuring five dynamic incident markers that refresh with each visit. Clicking on a marker reveals essential details such as date, location, and incident type, providing users with real-time information. The Prince George County map, highlighted in red, offers an exclusive feature; clicking anywhere on it provides a comprehensive breakdown of each incident, highlighting their monthly occurrences. For a quick overview, the home page also showcases the ten most recent incidents. Additionally, our dedicated help page serves as a user-friendly platform, allowing visitors to submit feedback and find contact information for further assistance. This design prioritizes user engagement, combining real-time incident updates with a responsive avenue for interaction and support.
